PadsWay needs two drivers by Nefarius to work. Install them BEFORE using PadsWay:

  - ViGEmBus (REQUIRED) - creates the virtual Xbox 360 controller
        https://github.com/nefarius/ViGEmBus/releases
        Validated version: ViGEmBus_1.22.0_x64_x86_arm64

  - HidHide (RECOMMENDED) - hides the physical controller from games
        https://github.com/nefarius/HidHide/releases
        Validated version: HidHide_1.5.230_x64

PadsWay controls them automatically once installed - you don't need to open
their interfaces by hand. This setup does NOT install the drivers for you.
